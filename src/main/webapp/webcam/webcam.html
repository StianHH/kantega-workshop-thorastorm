Webcam selfie stick

In this task you will use the getUserMedia API to connect your laptop's webcam to a video element. First you have to grant the browser access to the webcam. A popup should appear near the address bar.

Your goal is to:

  • Use the getUserMedia function to request a video stream from your webcam
  • In the success function: Set the video source by using HTMLMediaElement.srcObject.
  • Again, Google is your friend

Once you've got your video connected, you can:

  • Change the function animationLoop such that your video will play on the phone's screen
